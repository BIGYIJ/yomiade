About

Yomi Adeyemi (Yọmí Adé) blends raw lyricism with Yorùbá heritage, crafting a unique sound that bridges AfroFusion, Afro-HipHop, and Melodic Rap. Born in Philadelphia and raised in Canberra within a Nigerian household, Yomi’s music reflects a life lived between cultures — emotional, grounded, and deeply expressive.

Formerly known as BIGYIJ, his evolution marks a new chapter of authenticity and artistic growth. His debut EP, “Pieces of Me,” showcases his versatility and storytelling depth, intertwining faith, struggle, and identity into powerful sonic narratives. With an unmistakable voice and vision, Yọmí Adé stands poised to make a lasting mark on the global music scene.
(Not to be confused with Yomi Ade, the Nigerian gospel artist.)
